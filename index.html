Job Description

We are seeking a highly motivated Marketing Manager to join our team at Abhyaz. The successful candidate will be responsible for developing and executing marketing strategies to promote our products and services. Key responsibilities include:

  • Developing and implementing marketing plans
  • Managing social media accounts
  • Creating and managing email campaigns
  • Conducting market research and analyzing data
  • Collaborating with other departments to ensure consistent messaging

The ideal candidate will have a Bachelor's degree in Marketing, Communications, or related field, and at least 5 years of experience in marketing. Strong communication and organizational skills are a must.
